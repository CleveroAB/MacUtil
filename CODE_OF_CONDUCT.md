# Code Of Conduct

MacUtil should be a useful, low-drama project for people who care about native
macOS tools.

Please:

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Keep criticism specific to code, behavior, design, and documentation.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Respect that maintainers may decline broad features, risky dependencies, or
  changes that make the utility harder to trust.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that make the
project unsafe or unpleasant for others.
